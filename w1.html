Goals and Purpose

Our goal with this project is to save the lives of people who do not have the luxury of visiting a medical professional.
We hope to assist those in dire need by giving them the convenience of knowing what sort of illness they might have.

Preventative Care

With out cutting-edge technology, we have created a machine that can instantly compile and predict what kind of problems a patient has.

Responsive Care

Once a patient becomes ill, they can use our website to analyze and understand the dangers of their illness.
