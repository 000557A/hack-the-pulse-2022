What does the symptoms check do?

The symptoms check checks your symptoms and tells you how to deal with them and depending on the symptoms it may give you a posible illness you could have. To use the symptom checker, press the "green play button" towards the bottom right. Our goal with this simple program is to deliver information in a quick and timely manner.
